Cup Noodles

Easy and fast!

If you're out of time and need to eat something, just buy one of these cup noodles.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Cup noodle

Steps

  1. Boil water.
  2. Open half of the cup noodle lid and put the water to the mark inside and close again.
  3. Wait for approximately 3 minutes.
  4. Profit.
